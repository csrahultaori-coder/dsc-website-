FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)?
A Digital Signature Certificate is a government-recognised electronic equivalent of a physical signature, issued by a licensed Certifying Authority under the Information Technology Act, 2000. It is used to sign documents, file returns and authenticate identity on portals like MCA, GST, Income Tax, e-Tendering and DGFT.
How long does it take to get a new DSC?
In most cases we issue a Class 3 DSC within 30 minutes to 4 hours of completing Video KYC and document verification, subject to portal processing time. USB token delivery within Mumbai Metropolitan Region is typically same-day.
Which documents are required for a DSC application?
PAN card, Aadhaar card, a recent passport-size photograph and an active mobile number/email linked for OTP verification. Organisational DSCs additionally need incorporation documents, authorisation letter and organisational PAN.
Is Video KYC compulsory for DSC issuance?
Yes. As per Controller of Certifying Authorities (CCA) guidelines, Video KYC is a mandatory identity-verification step for all Class 3 individual and organisation DSCs. We assist you through the entire process remotely.
Do you provide DSC services outside Mumbai?
Yes, we serve clients Pan-India through our remote Video KYC process and courier-based USB token delivery, in addition to walk-in service at our Mira Road office.
Can foreign directors of Indian companies get a DSC?
Yes, we issue DSCs to foreign nationals and NRIs holding directorship in Indian companies. This requires apostilled/notarised documents as per the country of residence, which our team helps you arrange correctly.
What is the validity of a DSC and when should I renew it?
DSCs are typically issued for 2 years. We recommend starting the renewal process 15–20 days before expiry to avoid any disruption in filings, tender submissions or e-way bill generation.
Is my USB token FIPS 140-3 compliant?
We supply only genuine, CCA-approved FIPS 140-2/140-3 certified tokens from authorised manufacturers, ensuring full compatibility with MCA, GST and e-Tendering portals.
